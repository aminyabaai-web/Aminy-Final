# Aminy LLC — Contractor Confidentiality & IP Assignment Agreement

**MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AND INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT**

This Agreement is entered into as of _________________ ("Effective Date") by and
between:

**Aminy LLC**, an Arizona limited liability company ("Company"), and

**________________________________** ("Contractor").

---

## 1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by Company
to Contractor, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by inspection,
including but not limited to:

(a) **Source code**, algorithms, software architecture, database schemas, API
designs, and system configurations;

(b) **Trade secrets** including but not limited to: AI context injection methods,
provider matching scoring algorithms, clinical note generation pipelines,
treatment plan translation engines, EVV reconciliation systems, denial management
algorithms, engagement scoring models, care pathway routing systems, and
parent-child bidirectional data bridge architectures;

(c) **Business information** including pricing models, unit economics, customer
lists, provider networks, payer relationships, revenue data, growth metrics,
go-to-market strategies, and competitive analyses;

(d) **Clinical and regulatory information** including HIPAA compliance methods,
insurance coding databases, payer-specific denial guidance, and Medicaid waiver
integration strategies;

(e) **Product roadmaps**, feature specifications, design mockups, user research
data, and strategic planning documents.

## 2. OBLIGATIONS

Contractor agrees to:

(a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;

(b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior
written consent;

(c) Use Confidential Information solely for performing services for Company;

(d) Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon termination of the
engagement;

(e) Not reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any software or technology
provided by Company;

(f) Immediately notify Company of any unauthorized disclosure or use.

## 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT

(a) **Work Product.** All inventions, developments, improvements, designs, code,
algorithms, documentation, and other works of authorship created by Contractor
in connection with services for Company ("Work Product") shall be the sole and
exclusive property of Company.

(b) **Work Made for Hire.** To the extent permitted by law, all Work Product
shall be considered "works made for hire" as defined by the U.S. Copyright Act.

(c) **Assignment.** To the extent any Work Product is not a "work made for hire,"
Contractor hereby irrevocably assigns to Company all right, title, and interest
in and to such Work Product, including all patent rights, copyrights, trade
secret rights, and other intellectual property rights therein.

(d) **Moral Rights.** Contractor waives any and all moral rights in the Work
Product, including rights of attribution and integrity.

(e) **Further Assurance.** Contractor agrees to execute any documents and take
any actions reasonably requested by Company to perfect Company's ownership of
the Work Product, including patent and copyright applications.

(f) **Prior Inventions.** Contractor has listed on Exhibit A all prior inventions
relevant to the services. Anything not listed is presumed to be Company property
if created during the engagement.

## 4. NON-COMPETE (LIMITED)

During the engagement and for twelve (12) months following termination,
Contractor shall not:

(a) Develop, contribute to, or advise any product or service that directly
competes with Aminy's behavioral wellness platform for neurodivergent families;

(b) Solicit or recruit any employee or contractor of Company;

(c) Use Confidential Information to develop competing products.

## 5. NON-SOLICITATION

For twenty-four (24) months following termination, Contractor shall not directly
or indirectly solicit, hire, or engage any employee, contractor, provider, or
customer of Company with whom Contractor had contact during the engagement.

## 6. REMEDIES

Contractor acknowledges that breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm
to Company. In addition to any other legal remedies, Company shall be entitled to
seek injunctive relief without posting a bond.

## 7. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for five (5) years from the date of last
access to Confidential Information, or until all Confidential Information becomes
publicly available through no fault of Contractor.

## 8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Arizona, with
exclusive jurisdiction in Maricopa County, Arizona.

## 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding
confidentiality and IP assignment, superseding all prior agreements.
