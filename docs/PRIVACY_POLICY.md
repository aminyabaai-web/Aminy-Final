# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 28, 2026
**Last Updated:** January 28, 2026

## Introduction

Aminy ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your family. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web services (collectively, the "Service").

We understand that you're trusting us with sensitive information about your family, including your child's health and developmental information. We take this responsibility seriously and have implemented comprehensive safeguards to protect this information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Name and email address
- Password (stored securely with encryption)
- Phone number (optional, for notifications)

**Child Profile Information**
- Child's first name or nickname
- Date of birth
- Diagnoses and conditions
- Therapy information
- Communication preferences

**Health and Behavioral Data**
- Behavior logs and observations
- ABC (Antecedent-Behavior-Consequence) data
- Sleep patterns and quality
- Mealtime information
- Therapy session notes
- Goal progress tracking
- Caregiver stress levels

**Provider Information** (for healthcare providers)
- Professional credentials (BCBA, RBT, etc.)
- NPI number
- License information
- Organization affiliation

**Community Content**
- Posts and comments you create
- Messages with providers
- Feedback and surveys

### Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Information**
- Features you use and how often
- Session duration and timing
- Pages and screens viewed
- Search queries within the app

**Device Information**
- Device type and operating system
- App version
- Browser type (web)
- IP address (for security)

**We Do NOT Collect:**
- Precise location data
- Contact lists
- Photos (unless you upload them)
- Data from other apps on your device

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses

| Purpose | Data Used | Legal Basis |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Provide the Service | Account, child profiles, health data | Contract performance |
| AI-powered insights | Behavioral data, goals | Consent + legitimate interest |

## How We Share Your Information

### We May Share With:

**Healthcare Providers** (with your consent)
- BCBAs, therapists, or other providers you explicitly authorize
- Shared data includes: treatment plans, progress notes, behavioral data

- Cloud hosting (Supabase, AWS) - for data storage

- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for purposes other than providing the Service

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control

You have the right to:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Access your data** | Settings → Privacy → Download My Data |
| **Correct your data** | Edit directly in app or contact support |
| **Delete your data** | Settings → Privacy → Delete Account |
| **Export your data** | Settings → Privacy → Download My Data |
| **Restrict processing** | Contact privacy@aminy.care |
| **Object to processing** | Contact privacy@aminy.care |

### HIPAA Rights

As our Service handles Protected Health Information (PHI), you have additional rights under HIPAA:

- **Right to access:** Request copies of your health records
- **Right to amend:** Request corrections to your health records
- **Right to accounting:** Request a list of who has accessed your PHI
- **Right to restrict:** Request restrictions on how we use/disclose PHI
- **Right to confidential communications:** Request we communicate via specific methods

To exercise HIPAA rights, contact: privacy@aminy.care

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service.

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account information | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Health records | 7 years after last activity (HIPAA requirement) |
| Usage logs | 1 year |
| AI conversations | Until you delete (or account deletion) |
| Payment records | 7 years (tax requirements) |

After account deletion:
- Personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized/aggregated data may be retained
- Health records retained as required by law
