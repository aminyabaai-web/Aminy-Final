## 📐 Brand Architecture

### Three Value Pillars

**1. Calm & Predictability**
- Description: "Reduce stress through daily structure using gentle ABA-based routines."
- Application: Morning routines, visual schedules, predictable cues
- Color: Blue (Shield icon)

**2. Connection & Confidence**
- Description: "Empower parents and celebrate every bit of progress with your child."
- Application: Progress tracking, positive reinforcement, milestone celebrations
- Color: Green (Heart icon)

**3. Science & Simplicity**
- Description: "Behavioral insights grounded in proven ABA principles, no clinical jargon."
- Application: Reports, goals, analytics presented in parent-friendly language
- Color: Purple (Target icon)

### Language Framework

**USE These Words:**
- calm, connect, cue, progress, gentle, science-backed
- everyday, together, support, growth, celebration
- routine, structure, reinforcement, consistency
- guidance, adaptive, personalized, predictable, safe

**AVOID These Words:**
- therapy, diagnosis, disorder, prescription, patient
- treatment, intervention, clinical, cure, fix

**ABA-Specific Terms (Educational/Proof Contexts):**
- "Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)" (full form on first mention)
- "ABA principles"
- "behavioral science"
- "positive reinforcement"
- "gentle cues"
- "predictable routines"
- "evidence-informed"

### Tone & Voice System

**Voice:** Warm-expert — 60% compassionate coach, 40% intelligent assistant

**Characteristics:**
- Talk like texting a close friend who's a behavioral science expert
- Use contractions, natural pauses, conversational flow
- Show personality — warm, light humor when appropriate
- NEVER formal or robotic ("Great! To start..." = BAD)
- Always pair ABA with AI: "Powered by adaptive AI and grounded in ABA science"

**Examples:**

✅ **GOOD:**
- "Let's make mornings smoother together."
- "Small steps. Big calm."
- "Using ABA principles, Aminy helps create calm morning routines."
- "That's positive reinforcement working!"

❌ **BAD:**
- "Great! To start, please provide your child's first name."
- "We will now commence your treatment plan."
- "Your patient requires clinical intervention."
- "Please schedule therapy appointments."
